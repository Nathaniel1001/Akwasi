Ideas

OVERNIGHT TRIPS FOR THOSE STAYING BACK!!!

Bored of spending your Mid-Sem stuck on campus with nothing to do? LET ME SORT YOU OUT. My vision is... organised trips for students staying behind on campus, for you to actually enjoy your break. CULTURAL SITES, SWEET RESORTS, or maybe just a CHANGE OF SCENERY these trips will definitely make staying in school just as exciting as leaving. BECAUSE EVERY STUDENT DESERVES A MID-SEM TO REMEBER.

EXTENDING THE WIFI TO THE GIRLS CANOPY!

Relaxing at the girl's canopy with your friends, but... THE WIFI IS NON-EXISTENT!? This issue has been an inconvenience for students for a while, as they struggle with the shakiness or lack of a Wi-Fi connection while trying to relax, raise money, or get things done. LET ME SORT YOU OUT! I want to bring Wi-Fi extenders to the girl's canopy, so we can study, play music, and have fun under the girl's canopy. Let's make every student space enjoyable.

INSTALLING STADIUM LIGHTS ON THE BIG BASKETBALL COURT AND TRACK FIELD!!!

Imagine playing basketball with friends, but it gets dark, and you can’t see the rim! Let’s fix that by installing stadium lights on the court and track, making sports safer and more accessible at any time. Whether it’s a morning run or a Saturday night game, let’s keep the action going!

HOW DO I HIT MY CARDIO GOALS

Tired of broken treadmills and old-fashioned gym equipment hindering your body goals? LET ME SORT YOU OUT. My vision is... AN UPGRADED GYM with newer, more reliable machines that can finally keep up with us - including a new STAIRMASTER to STEP-UP your cardio! A better gym means, BETTER WORKOUTS, FASTER FITNESS PROGRESS, BETTER HEALTH, and A BETTER SOS-HGIC

REPLACING THE BLUE CANOPY TO WHITE TO REDUCE HEAT

The blue canopy traps heat, making it unbearably hot, even with fans. Replacing it with a white canopy would reflect light and significantly reduce heat buildup. This simple change will create a cooler, more comfortable space for everyone to focus, eat, and socialize!.

More fans

Ghana’s heat is no joke, and our rooms? Even worse. Picture this—you’re in Titan or Mano, trying to focus, but the fan is nowhere near your desk. Instead of studying, you’re just battling sweat while prepping for that big math test. Simple fix: ceiling fans in study areas. Less heat, more focus, and better grades. Sounds like a win, right?
